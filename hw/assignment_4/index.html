How to Compose Music

Materials

  • Desk or Writing Surface
  • Piece of Manuscript Paper
  • Pen or Pencil
  • Keyboard or Other Instrument

These instructions include the fundamental steps to begin writing your own music and songs.

  1. Step 1: Prepare Your Manuscript Paper

    Grab your manuscript paper and take a seat at your desk or table. It's time to begin.

  2. Step 2: Come Up With a Melody

    Take to your piano and pluck out a short melody. The more simple, the better!

  3. Step 3: Write Your Melody Down

    Take that melody to paper and maybe come up with some lyrics (if that's your thing).

  4. Step 4: Harmonise

    Add some harmony to that melody. Throw some fancy chords in there and make it sound pretty (or sad, dark, confused, etc.).
